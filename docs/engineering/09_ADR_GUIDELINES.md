# Architecture Decision Records (ADR) Guidelines

## Purpose
Business requirements drive architecture. Architecture never exists for its own sake.
Any significant architectural change must be preceded by an ADR.

## Content
An ADR must include:
- **Title:** A clear, concise title.
- **Context:** Why is this decision necessary?
- **Decision:** The proposed solution and implementation plan.
- **Consequences:** The positive and negative impacts of the decision (Dependencies, Security, Performance).
- **Alternatives Considered:** Why were other approaches rejected?

## Governance
If a requested feature conflicts with the existing architecture, STOP.
Explain the conflict, propose the minimal change via an ADR, and wait for explicit approval.
